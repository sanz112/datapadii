About us

Padi tokens are a loyalty, incentive and rewards token for selected African markets and diaspora. Padi token will be built on the block chain as a L2 protocol on top of the CELO block chain. Padi token will focus on data and airtime buyers, students, vendors, agents, informal traders etc.

At Datapadi our mission is 3-fold:
  • To reward our loyal customers of data and airtime with padi tokens
  • To create a robust and engaging community of padi token holders
  • To create employment opportunities for unemployed youths, students, POS/ banking Agents, Esusu Agents and developers through our affiliate program
